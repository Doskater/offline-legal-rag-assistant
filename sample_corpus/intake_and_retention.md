# Client Intake And Retention Policy

## Conflict Checks

Every new matter must include a conflict check before substantive work begins. The intake record should show the client name, opposing parties, related entities, date of review, reviewer initials, and the result of the conflict check.

## Matter File Retention

Closed client matter files should be retained for seven years unless a longer period is required by law, client agreement, litigation hold, or firm policy. The retention date should be recorded when the matter is closed.

## Long-Form Writing

Articles and book chapters may draw from the research corpus, but the writer must keep source notes showing which passages support each major section.
