# Vendor Agreement Notes

## Termination For Breach

The standard vendor agreement permits termination after a material breach if the breaching party fails to cure the issue within ten days after written notice. The notice should identify the breached obligation, describe the facts, and state the requested cure.

## Limitation Of Liability

The draft limitation of liability clause excludes indirect damages, lost profits, and consequential losses. The cap is usually tied to fees paid during the prior twelve months, except for confidentiality violations, payment obligations, and intentional misconduct.

## Service Level Escalation

If a service level is missed twice in a calendar month, the account lead must notify the operations manager. The vendor must provide a corrective action plan within five business days.
